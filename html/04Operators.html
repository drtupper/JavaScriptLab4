Page 4: Operators

Operators are special symbols that designate that some sort of computation should be performed.

JavaScript has ten different operators:

  • + is the addition operator
  • - is the subtraction operator
  • = is the assignment operator
  • * is the multiplication operator
  • / is the division operator
  • % is the modulus (calculate remainder) operator
  • >, <, and == are comparison operators
  • ** is the exponentiation operator
  • ++ is the increment operator
  • -- is the decrement operator

There are also combination operators such as +=, *=, etc., but we will learn those after we learn about variables.
